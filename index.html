Because we’re going further:

Electricity is included.

Thanks to our unique Clyde Energy Benefit, electricity is included both at public charging stations and at home or at work.

When you charge your electric car at one of the many public charging stations in our partner charging network in Switzerland and throughout Europe, the electricity is included in your monthly subscription.

Private charging is credited in the form of vouchers that you can put towards your next monthly payment.

We are more sustainable.

With the electric car subscription from Clyde, we are working together to improve our carbon footprint and contribute to Swiss climate goals (zero emissions goal). How do we do that?

With its 100% electric fleet (from 2024), Clyde produces zero exhaust emissions. You can reduce your CO2 footprint with an efficient electric car.

Let’s do something good for the environment together and enjoy electrifying driving fun at the same time!

We are cheaper.

It is on average over 15% cheaper from a total cost perspective to subscribe to one of the most popular electric cars with Clyde than to finance comparable ICEVs via leasing or buying on a loan.

We are the only subscription where all costs are included in the monthly rate: taxes, insurance, servicing, tyres, additional drivers and the like.

You don’t even have to worry about the electricity price because, with us, public charging is included and private charging is credited with CHF 0.30/kWh in the form of vouchers.

We are simpler.

Our prices are 100% transparent. With Clyde, you only pay a fixed price – with no nasty surprises. Switching to electric mobility with Clyde has never been easier and is risk-free.

We offer you maximum flexibility and independence, and adapt to your needs.

We are more dynamic.

Our 100% electric fleet (from 2024 onwards) features the latest electric cars from VW, Audi, Škoda and others. Every single model guarantees maximum driving fun thanks to state-of-the-art performance and technology. Full torque is available from the first metre, so even our small electric cars are more than convincing with their sporty acceleration.

Experience the unique dynamism that vehicles with internal combustion engines cannot offer. Once an electric car, always an electric car!

What requirements do I have to fulfil to be able to use Clyde?

At least 20 years old, residency in Switzerland oder Liechtenstein, valid driving licence (category B) and a successful credit check. If you fit these criteria, you’re a match. If not, your dream car has to stay with us for a little longer.

Is delivery to your door really for free?

Yes. Yes. And yes again. Clyde’s all about keeping things transparent. We don’t have any hidden costs. Life is full of important moments – we want you to have the time and freedom to enjoy them. How? By delivering your car to your door and picking it up once your subscription has ended.

How can Clyde promise 10 days delivery time?

For cars in stock, once we receive your payment, we pull out all the stops to provide you with the best service and a unique experience. We start the process of car preparation and registration and charm the lovely people at the local registration offices in your canton of residence so you can be cruising the streets in 10 working days. The quickest way to get your Clyde is to choose a car that is in stock, select Home Delivery when booking and pay by credit card. For cars that are not immediately available from stock, we will contact you as soon as the car arrives and then deliver your car to you in just 10 working days.

What is Clyde?

Clyde is more than just the most flexible electric car subscription in Switzerland. We’re the only electric car subscription that includes everything – even electricity at all public charging stations in our partner network; and now even private charging costs are credited. We are also the only provider that does not charge a starting fee and even delivers your electric car free of charge to anywhere in Switzerland.

It’s never been easier to have your own electric car to drive. The entire booking process is 100% digital. Clyde customers choose the electric car that suits them and select the desired minimum subscription period of between 3 and 48 months, as well as the monthly mileage package of between 250 km and 4,000 km.

Why do I not have to pay a starting fee?

It’s easy: because Clyde is the only provider that really offers everything included. Of course, this also includes the starting fee.
